ParkingLot

1. Parking lot has multiple parking floors.
2. Each parking floor has multiple parking slots.
3. Parking lot has multiple entry and exit points.
4. Parking lot has display panel to show the status of parking spots.
5. Parking lot has a ticket machine to issue parking tickets.
6. Ticket will be paid by cash or credit card at the exit point.
7. The system should not allow more vehicles than the capacity of parking lot.
8. If the parking lot is full, then the vehicle should not be allowed to enter and should display on the display panel.
9. Parking spot can be of different sizes (small, compact, large etc).
10. Vehicle can be of different types (car, bus, truck etc).
11. Parking can be chargeable on hourly basis.
